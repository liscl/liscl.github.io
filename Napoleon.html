Coordinated Multiple View - Berezina River Crossing

This were the CMVs I programmed during my Master Thesis. I used them for an empirical study to analyse the effectiveness of uncertainty visualization in CMVs. As a case study the dataset of the Berezina crossing during Napoleon's Campaign in Russia 1812 has been used.


Tools: D3.js, HTML, CSS
